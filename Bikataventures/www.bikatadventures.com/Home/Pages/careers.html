Why work with Bikat Adventures?

Adventure Exposure:

No matter what team you are in, we encourage our team members to take time off and explore the adventures we hold. We believe All Work and All Play makes for an exciting life.

Freedom to Experiment:

You have the freedom to conceptualize and experiment with new projects in your existing role and try your hand at new roles. We have had Content Writers become Assistant Trek Leaders, Designers become On-field Photographers and so much more.

Good Work Life Balance

Work is important but so is your life outside of it. We love watching our team members pursue passions and hobbies outside of work hours and go out of our way to help them in their learning journeys.

No Hierarchy

Here, everyone plays a role at a time. The CEO has become the belayer and the COO has been assistant to the Expedition Leader. We are not suggesting chaos where we donâ€™t know who does what but we wear different hats as the situation demands and once you are there with a hat, you wear it with pride.

No Micro-Management

We do not believe in micro-management. It hampers creativity, dampens the spirit and makes for bad work culture. Once you are on a project, we encourage you to own it. And if you need any help along the way, we are always here.

Excellent Growth Opportunity

With freedom to experiment comes great exposure, learning and growth. Our people have grown from helpers to cooks to mountaineers, from content writers to heading marketing and communications, from designers to social media executives and so much more.
