Bikat Adventures

Hi! Bikat Adventures is a learning-based Adventure Organization. Our endeavour is to make the outdoors exciting, informing and accessible to everyone in India. To that end, Bikat Adventures was founded in 2010 when we held our first guided trek to Har Ki Dun in Uttarakhand. Since then, we have grown to operate on a whopping 80 Trails across the country. High-altitude Himalayan treks, Guided Mountaineering Expeditions and Himalayan Cycling Expeditions are three of our specialties and what we are best known and celebrated for. Alongside this, we have begun branching out recently, adding skiing courses, cycling and wildlife tours into our already vast portfolio. Our Vision in the long run is simple. We want to take you on Adventures that make a difference, adventures that transform your moments into memories and make the world a more interesting place to live in :)
