Geethanjali Jujjavarapu

Lawyer by education, Geethanjali loves travelling and documenting her travels through photographs and blogs. Having travelled abroad and in India, she enjoys travelling by herself and spending time with the locals where ever she goes. She is a PADI certified advanced open water diver and the ocean is her go to place. Although an ocean baby at heart, Geethanjali makes her way to the mountains every once in a while to soak in the sheer grandeur that they have to offer. She is also a painter and a cinephile who is very eager to deconstruct films with anyone who is willing to indulge!
